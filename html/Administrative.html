Administrative : House Career Path

The Administrative Career Path outlines roles, responsibilities, skills, sample career options, and associated training opportunities for staff who are interested in important operational functions of a House office. Click the button to the right to view an overview of what career development looks like within this path.

The career path below is interactive. Please click on the boxes or arrows to find out more about the specific position or additional information about moving from one position to another. Dotted lines represent more challenging career movements.

Note: The content presented is intended for developmental purposes only. Position titles, paths, and opportunities in individual Member offices may differ.
