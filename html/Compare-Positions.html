House Career Paths

The House Human Resources Hub's career paths are designed as a developmental resource specifically for staff within the U.S. House of Representatives. The pathing tools outline the unique roles, responsibilities, skills, sample career options, and associated training opportunities for 16 positions within the Member office community. Each of the 16 positions align within one of four main career tracks (Administrative, Communications, District, and Legislative).

All House staff are invited to use these resources to explore career possibilities and to develop short-and-longer-range individual developmental training plan. To get started, view the overall mapping, explore specific career paths, or compare different positions via the buttons on the right.

Note: The content presented is intended for developmental purposes only. Position titles, paths, and opportunities in individual Member offices may differ.
